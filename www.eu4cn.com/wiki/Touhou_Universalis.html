“欢迎来到幻想乡”

特性：

- 新的幻想乡区域有98个省份（它们中的一些是我瞎起的名字），
- 新的东方系列文化组有一堆文化和3个东方系列宗教，
- 24个可以游玩的国家（1个可成立），
- 东方系列读取屏幕，
- 2个新的政体，
- 思潮传播按省份发展度：
>1点每年 > 20发展
>2点每年 > 30发展
>5点每年 > 40发展
- 3个新的贸易节点

将会增加的：

- 更多的剧本书签和幻想乡的历史记录
- 各个国家的国家理念
- 东方系列角色顾问肖像
- 随机和故事事件：
>鬼族留在旧都还是返回山上
>鬼人正邪的下克上
>月人入侵的重大异变
- 最重要国家的任务，
- 可能还有一些我忘了的或者来自建议的事情。

下一个主要更新将会包括国家理念。

为了更好的体验，我推荐东方管弦乐Mod： https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=618499801&searchtext=touhou

一些东西诸如省份名称，国家的发展度或者位置可能和东方系列的官方设定不同（我希望没有人会为此恨我）但是大部分都是有意设计的特性（MOST NOT ALL）为了确保玩起来不坏也不无聊，比如那些“通用”省份——省份越多越好！

虽然这个项目主要是我学习如何在EU IV里编辑地图，我会一直支持它只要我能，因为我是东方系列的大粉丝！

大部分国旗来自CK2的"Kingdom of gensokyo" Mod，所以我没做它们（如果你对此有任何问题请告诉我）

为了更新（请） 评分，评论以及提出建议

描述

这个Mod是来自Taohao的《Touhou Universalis》的更新。

在多次更新版本的请求之后，他给我许可来上传一个该Mod的新版本。

你能在这里找到原Mod： https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1448785872&searchtext=touhou 所有荣誉属于他。All credits go to him.

以下描述来自于 "Touhou Universalis" 

"Welcome to the land of fantasy"

Features:
- New Gensokyo region with 98 provinces (some of them are generic ones terribly named by me),
- New Touhou culture group with a few cultures and 3 Touhou religions,
- 24 playable countries (1 formable), *now 24 playable countries, 2 formable and 1 releasable*
- Touhou Loading screens,
- 2 new government types.
- Institution spread in developed provinces:
>1 point a year > 20 dev
>2 point a year > 30 dev
>5 point a year > 40 dev
- 3 new trade nodes

To be added:
- More bookmarks and progresion of history in Gensokyo,
- Advisor portraits with touhou characters,
- Random and story events:
>Oni in either former capital or returning to the mountain
>Seija's rebelion
>Major crisis of lunarian invasion
- Missions for most important countries,
- Maybe something more i forgot or from Suggestions.

For better experience i reccomend The touhou orchestral music mod:
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=618499801&searchtext=touhou

Some things like province names, development or placement of countries can differ from Touhou canon (I hope noone is going to hate me for this) but most of these are a full intentional features (MOST NOT ALL) made to secure not bad and not boring gameplay
for instance the "Generic" provinces - The more provinces the better!
Altho this project was mainly me learning how to edit map in EU IV i will support it as long as i can because i am a huge fan of Touhou!

Most of the country flags are from "Kingdom of gensokyo" mod for ck2 so i didn't made them (tell me if you have any issue with that)^

我正在做一个更大的幻想乡地图，以便让更多的角色可以作为他们的国家登场，有更多的空间去做有影响力的事件以及让我自己有更多创作余地。我会在某天发布一个beta版本到dropbox。如果进展顺利完整的Mod将会用更大的幻想乡更新，带有更多的省份和国家。更小的版本之后会在dropbox。

改动说明

 更新于：2月4日 下午11:08

General:
- Touhou Updated is now updated for patch 1.28.3

Balance changes:
Chireiden:
- Swapped the ideas "Nuclear Energy" and "Subconscious Control"
- Increased the reduced liberty desire through "Subconscious Control" from 2.5 to 15
NOTE:
Satori isn't a person who would fight personally if not really necessary. Instead she uses mind reading abilites to manipulate others to fight her enemies. Thus Chireiden is the "Austria of Gensokyo" and uses mostly diplomacy to let other wage wars. To make this play style more attractive the ideas got swapped and the reduced liberty desire got buffed.
For the AI Chireiden it changes basically nothing because the AI despises making vassals.
- Gensokyo is now an own continent, which allows to build trade companies in China, India and in the Spice Isles
- Added a new trade route from Beijing to Moriya Shrine to make the effect of trade companies in China more worth

Flavor:
- Some gensokyan provinces change their name if a german country controls them.
(NOTE: not all provinces will get a new name and it's only german countries because I personally can only write in german and english names for provinces. When somebody wants to help out please text me on steam.)
- Touhou Religions make now sounds if you start to convert provinces, click on the religion tab or claim the Defender of Faith. Also there will be now a real missionary in a converting province.
- If you play the mod without "Rights of Man" you will get an event which replaces every old, ex-immortal ruler in Gensokyo with a drastically younger version. This should prevent your ruler dying this early in the game. It should be noted that if you activate afterward "Rights of Man" these rulers don't become immortal again.

Bugfixes
- Fixed a bug which caused to lose the restoration of union CB for Scarlet Realm and Chireiden when they fulfill the required mission
- Hakurei's idea "Hakurei festivals" gives now global taxes bonus
- Cirnia, Hakurei- and Moriya clan have for non-Dharma users now their unique government form
- Now only genoskyan nations without unique national ideas get the gensokyan ideas

更新于：1月19日 上午3:40

New content:
- new missions for Chireiden
- a new tag for Chireiden and Scarlett Realm
- new culture: Fairy
- a new, releasable tag with fairy culture: Cirnia (led by Cirno with an event)
- new events for japanese nations when they get foot on Gensokyo
- there are now alliances at the beginning of the game

Bug fixes:
- fixed a missing localization for an unit type
- fixed a bug where the institutions didn't gave bonus for trade companies

Improved features:
- major governments reforms (for example Scarlett Army reform) are now usuable for non-Dharma users at tech 10

更新于：2018年12月19日 上午3:43

- a new tech group for gensokyan nations (thanks Galadorn for helping)

- new government reforms
- new government form for Hakurei Clan and Moriya Clan
- new experimental mission tree for Scarlett Realm
depending on feedback there will be more missions tree in this style added

Changes I made:
- Australia and Panama tradenodes are now connected with Gensokyo to make colonizing more appealing
